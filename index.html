About the Game

Glyphara is an offline word path puzzle where players trace hidden words through glowing letter tiles. The game blends focused word-search logic with a quiet fantasy atmosphere, progressive levels, saved progress, star ratings, and music controls.

Features

30 progressively challenging offline levels.
Horizontal and vertical word paths in every puzzle.
Star ratings, replayable levels, and saved progress.
Atmospheric visuals designed around luminous glyph tiles.
Background music and sound effect toggles.
No account required for play.
