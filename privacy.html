Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Glyphara ("the App") handles information when you use the game.

Information We Collect

Glyphara is designed as an offline single-player game and does not require you to create an account. The App may store gameplay progress, star ratings, level completion, music settings, and sound settings locally on your device.

The App may include advertising and security SDKs. These SDKs may process device information, advertising identifiers where permitted, approximate usage data, diagnostic information, and ad interaction data to provide ads, measure performance, prevent fraud, and maintain service security.

App Tracking Transparency

Where required by iOS, the App may ask for permission to track activity across apps and websites owned by other companies. If you decline, the App will continue to work, but ads may be less relevant.

How Information Is Used

  • To save your game progress and settings on your device.
  • To show, load, measure, and improve advertising placements.
  • To help detect abuse, fraud, or technical issues.
  • To improve gameplay stability and user experience.

Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party advertising, analytics, and security services. These providers may collect and process information under their own privacy policies. Their processing may include ad delivery, frequency capping, attribution, fraud prevention, and diagnostics.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared with service providers only as needed to operate ads, security features, diagnostics, and related app functionality, or when required by law.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Data Retention

Local gameplay data remains on your device unless you delete the App or reset progress. Data processed by third-party services is retained according to those providers' policies and applicable law.

Your Choices

You can disable music and sound in the App settings. You can manage tracking permission in iOS Settings. You may reset gameplay progress within the App where available, or delete the App to remove locally stored data.
